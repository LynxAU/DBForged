# Dragon Ball Game Features Research

## Summary
Research into Dragon Ball video games (DBZ: Kakarot, Dragon Ball FighterZ, DB Legends, Xenoverse) to identify features that would enhance DBForged.

---

## 1. COMBAT SYSTEMS

### From DBZ: Kakarot
- **Community Board**: Substories with characters that build relationships
- **Training Grounds**: Mini-games for skill XP (flying, ki control)
- **Battle Navigation**: Semi-open world with enemies roaming
- **Combo System**: String together basic attacks into combos

### From FighterZ
- **Assist System**: Call partner for attack support
- **Switch Mechanics**: Swap between characters mid-combo
- **Dragon Rush**: Unblockable grab mechanic
- **Vanish**: Quick dash behind opponent

### From Xenoverse
- **PQS (Parallel Quest System)**: Procedural missions
- **Skill Customization**: Mix and match skills
- **Mentor System**: Learn techniques from NPCs
- **Awoken Skills**: Transformation as ultimate abilities

### RECOMMENDED FOR DBFORGED:
- [ ] **Combo System**: Chain attacks together for bonuses
- [ ] **Training Mini-games**: Flying practice, ki control
- [ ] **Community/Relationship System**: Build bonds with NPCs
- [ ] **Mentor Learning**: Learn techniques from trainers

---

## 2. PROGRESSION SYSTEMS

### From DB Legends
- **Soul Boost**: Enhance cards with other cards
- **Limit Break**: Transcend max level
- **Equip System**: Attach items to boost stats
- **Z-Battle**: Difficulty scaling events

### From Xenoverse
- **Expert Mission**: Endgame content with scaling
- **Conton City**: Hub world with activities
- **TP Medal Shop**: Currency from missions

### RECOMMENDED FOR DBFORGED:
- [ ] **Soul/Equipment Enhancement**: Upgrade gear with materials
- [ ] **Limit Break System**: Push beyond max limits
- [ ] **Expert Missions**: Scaling endgame content
- [ ] **Conton-style Hub**: Central town with activities

---

## 3. TRANSFORMATION SYSTEMS

### Implemented Well:
- **Kaioken Stacking**: Already have x2 per second hold (x20 max)
- **Ultra Instinct**: Auto-trigger on low HP
- **Grade System**: SSJ variants (Grade 2, 3, Grade 4)

### Missing from Games:
- **Super Vegeta 2**: Quick dash + power
- **Mastered Ultra Instinct**: Full power UI
- **Beast/Golden Freeza**: Newer transformations
- **TULP**: Potential future forms

### RECOMMENDED FOR DBFORGED:
- [ ] **Transformation Unlocking**: Progress to unlock forms
- [ ] **Transformation Mastery**: Full meter = mastery level
- [ ] **Beast Form**: For Earthlings
- [ ] **TULP**: Potential transformation
